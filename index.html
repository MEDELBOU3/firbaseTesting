Answers to Your Burning Questions

AuraStream is your ultimate entertainment command center. It helps you discover movies and TV shows across various streaming services using real-time data, track what you've watched, manage your watchlist, and explore related content. Think of it as a super-powered TV guide and discovery tool.

No, AuraStream itself doesn't host or stream video content due to legal and licensing reasons. We primarily focus on discovery and tracking. We provide information and potentially deep links (where available and permitted) to the actual streaming services where you can watch the content (like Netflix, Hulu, etc.), assuming you have a subscription.

The core features presented on this landing page – discovery, tracking, watchlist management – are based on freely available APIs (like TMDb). This demonstration itself is free. Future development might include premium features, but the goal is to keep core discovery accessible.

We primarily use The Movie Database (TMDb) API for movie/show metadata, posters, cast information, ratings, and trending data. This product uses the TMDb API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDb. All data usage complies with TMDb's terms of service.

The community features (user reviews, lists, following others) mentioned in the 'Features' section are conceptual for this landing page demonstration and are not currently implemented. Building a full community requires significant backend infrastructure.
